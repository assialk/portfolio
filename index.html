About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate coder who's been programming since I was 8 years old, diving into different languages and exploring the endless possibilities of tech. My journey began with small projects, which eventually led to presenting my work to none other than Sébastien Missoffe, CEO of Google France — an experience that truly fueled my love for innovation.

Over the years, I've expanded my skills through experiences like attending Tumo Paris 1, a digital school where I learned to build video games in C# and create animations in JavaScript. I've also participated in unique programs, including the 42 piscine discovery, which introduced me to shell scripting, and "Girls Can Code," where I learned to code on microprocessors. Today, my focus is on crafting impactful, inclusive digital experiences and continually learning through new projects and collaborations.

Some Things I've Built

Project One: Coffee Study

The Coffee Study Tracker is a cozy, coffee-themed web app that helps users stay focused during study sessions. Featuring a countdown timer, a coin reward system, and animated sparkles rising from a coffee cup. For each minute of studying, users earn coins, and progress is saved even if the page is refreshed.
